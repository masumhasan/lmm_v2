# LearnMyMind - Feature List

LearnMyMind is a cognitive laboratory designed for mental muscle building and attention training. Unlike traditional therapy or meditation apps, it focuses on the structural training of attention.

## 1. Onboarding & Philosophy
- **Introductory Protocol**: A guided introduction to the "LearnMyMind" philosophy, distinguishing cognitive training from therapy or motivation.
- **Interactive Introduction**: Uses high-quality Lottie animations to explain the three core training modalities.
- **Expectation Management**: Clearly defines the app's scope as a tool for attention training, not emotional support or medical advice.

## 2. Cognitive Workouts (Train)
- **Structured Sessions**: Multiple workout modules (Workout 0 through 4) designed to strengthen specific cognitive functions.
- **Repetition-Based Training**: Workouts are built around "reps," emphasizing the importance of consistency and repetition in mental training.
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual indicators (circular progress bars) and feedback for completed reps and workouts.
- **Workout Library**: A categorized list of available training modules with descriptions of their cognitive focus.

## 3. Return To Now (Reset Protocol)
- **RTN-01 Protocol**: A neutral attention recalibration exercise designed to pull the user back into the present moment.
- **Multi-Sensory Grounding**:
    - **Visual Recalibration**: Prompts to identify and focus on objects in the immediate environment.
    - **Audio Recalibration**: Exercises to notice and isolate sounds.
    - **Physical Recalibration**: Guidance on noticing physical sensations and contact points.
- **Non-Emotional Focus**: Specifically designed to reset attention without attempting to change the user's emotional state.

## 4. Drift (Psychological Mirroring)
- **Pattern Exposure**: A feed of curated images and text fragments designed for psychological mirroring.
- **Interactive Media**: Images transition from grayscale to color upon interaction, symbolizing focused attention.
- **Lab-Style Interface**: Content is presented with reference IDs and analysis statuses, reinforcing the "Cognitive Laboratory" theme.

## 5. Neural Assistant (AI Coach)
- **Architectural Preview**: An interactive interface for a future real-time cognitive intervention system.
- **System Feedback**: Displays system status (e.g., "System Standby") and provides context for the training modules.
- **Foundational Guidance**: Provides introductory messages to help users calibrate their baseline training.

## 6. Muscle Dashboard & Analytics
- **Mental Muscle Visualization**: A dashboard tracking the strength and development of different cognitive "muscles."
- **Training Summary**: Overview of completed workouts, total reps, and training consistency.
- **Progress Analytics**: Data-driven insights into the user's cognitive training journey.

## 7. Profile & User State
- **Persistent Progress**: Saves user statistics, completed workouts, and onboarding status.
- **Training History**: A log of past activities and achievements.
- **Personalized Experience**: Adapts the interface based on the user's progress and training level.

## 8. Premium Access
- **Advanced Modules**: Preview and access to specialized training protocols.
- **Neural Assistant Full Access**: Future integration for real-time physiological and psychological data analysis.
- **Subscription Management**: Integrated paywall for managing premium features.
